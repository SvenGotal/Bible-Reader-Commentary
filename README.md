# BIBLIJA&ja

BIBLIJA&ja a.k.a. Bible-Reader-Commentary is a Croatian based web application where registered users can add their own insights and thoughts on a desired Bible chapter.
These insights are added in a form of a comment that can be defined as either private (for personal edification) or public for everyone to read and enjoy. This is primarily a Bible study tool but it's template could be adjusted for any kind of book whatsoever.  
## Why do this?
The main motivation of it's author is to motivate netizens to read God's word and see it's simplicity and deep treasure trove of wisdom and guidance in the modern world that it is.  
Enjoy the world's best seller in depth, discern the deep meanings meant for you to find and enrich your life with the wisdom from none other than the Author of Life Himself - **God.**
